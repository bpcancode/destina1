OUR HISTORY

BACKGROUND & HISTORY

Destina1 International is a Malaysian company using a multi-level marketing model to sell various Advance Scalar Energised products, primarily health and beauty. Destina1 International is the World Leader in Advance Scalar Energised Products and owns the patent rights for Advance Scalar Technology globally.

Founding and Early Years

Destina1 International was founded by Dato' Sri Dr Barani Karunakaran & Prof Dr Karunakaran in 2012. Both founders were father and son who started their business from their homes in Penang, Malaysia.

Growth and Expansion

Destina1 International experienced a substantial increase in the early years and quickly expanded its product line to include health products, beauty products, and food supplements. By 2013, Destina1 had gone international, opening markets in Australia, the UK, South Africa, Russia, Poland, and Turkey.

Global Expansion

The company became international in 2015, expanding into Asia and North America. Today, it promotes its Socia Entrepreneurship platform and Advance Scalar Energised Products in more than 78 countries worldwide.

Business Model

Destina1's business model is based on a direct sales business model. The product sales are made through traditional retail channels and are sold by independent distributors (IDs) directly to end consumers. IDs can also refer another distributor to sell Destina1 products, earning a percentage of their distributor's sales.

Products

Destina1's product line has expanded to include many goods. Their offerings include the popular D1 Advance Scalar Energised Pendant, D1 Advance Scalar Energised Alkaline Flask and Alkaline Stick, D1 Anti-Radiation Biomatrix Shield, D1 Scalarvia, D1 Scalar AFA, D1 Instant Coffee Premix 5 in 1, have become widely recognised and contributed significantly to the company's growth.

Business Model

Destina1's business model is based on direct selling and multi-level marketing. Rather than selling products through traditional retail channels, Destina1's products are sold by Independent Distributors (IDs) directly to end consumers. IDs can also recruit others to sell Destina1 products, earning a percentage of their recruits' sales.
